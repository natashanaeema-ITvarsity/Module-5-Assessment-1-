Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system at nearly 11 times the size of Earth and 317 times its mass.

The planet Jupiter is the fifth planet out from the Sun, and is two and a half times more massive than all the other planets in the solar system combined. It is made primarily of gases and is therefore known as a gas giant.

Jupiter facts

  • Diameter: 142.984 km
  • Mass: 1,19 x 10 27 kg
  • Moons: none
  • Orbit distance: 5,204 AU
  • Orbit period: 11,86 years
  • Surface temperature: -145°
  • First record: 1831
  • Recorded by: Astronomer named Samuel Heinrich Schwabe
